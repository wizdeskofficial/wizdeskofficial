To optimize system resources and maintain data quality, teams that remain inactive for 365 days will be automatically and permanently deleted.

Definition of Inactivity

A team is considered inactive when no team-related activities occur for 365 consecutive days, including:

  • No user logins from team members
  • No task creation, updates, or completions
  • No team member additions or management actions
  • No administrative activities by team leaders

Deletion Process

  • 30-day warning: We'll send email notifications before deletion
  • Complete removal: All team data, tasks, and member associations are permanently deleted
  • No recovery: Once deleted, team data cannot be recovered
  • Member accounts: Individual user accounts remain active but lose team association

Preventing Deletion

To keep your team active, simply log in and perform any team-related activity at least once per year. Regular usage ensures your data remains preserved.
