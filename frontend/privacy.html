Important: By using WizDesk, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Profile Information: Optional profile details you choose to provide

Usage Information

  • Task Data: Tasks, subtasks, assignments, progress, and deadlines
  • Team Activity: Member interactions, task assignments, completion rates
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage patterns

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our task management services

  • Monitor usage patterns and platform performance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues

Data Retention & Auto-Deletion

⚠️ Important: Team Auto-Deletion Policy

To maintain system performance and data hygiene, teams that show no activity for 365 consecutive days will be automatically deleted from our system.

What constitutes "activity"?

  • User logins to the platform
  • Task creation, updates, or completion
  • Team member additions or approvals
  • Any team-related actions within the platform

What happens when a team is deleted?

  • All team data including tasks, subtasks, and member associations are permanently removed
  • Team members' accounts are preserved but their team association is removed
  • Team leaders can create new teams or join existing ones
  • No backup or recovery is available after auto-deletion

How to prevent auto-deletion?

Simply log in and perform any team-related activity at least once every 365 days. We'll send reminder emails before deletion occurs.
